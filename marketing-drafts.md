## Product Hunt

### Tagline (60 chars max)
The simplest reminder app for iPhone — free, no ads, offline

### Description
**Snooze It** is a reminder app that does one thing well: remind you later.

**3 reminder modes:**
- **Countdown** — "Remind me in 30 minutes"
- **Moment** — "Tonight when I get home"
- **Recurring** — "Every 2 hours to drink water"

**Key features:**
- Safari Share Extension — save articles for later reading
- Home Screen Widgets — quick add or see your next reminder
- Notification actions — snooze 30 min or mark done, right from the notification
- Categories — organize by Work, Life, Read Later, or custom

**Privacy first:**
Zero data collection. No analytics. No ads. No accounts. Everything stays on your device.

**Free.** No subscription, no in-app purchases. Just a simple, useful tool.

### Maker Comment
Hi Product Hunt! I built Snooze It because I kept forgetting small things — articles to read later, messages to reply to, reminders to drink water. I didn't want a full task manager, just something that pops up at the right time.

It's free, has zero tracking, and works completely offline. Would love to hear what you think!

### Title
[Free] Snooze It — a minimal reminder app with recurring reminders, widgets, and Safari sharing

### Body
I just released **Snooze It**, a free reminder app for iPhone. No ads, no subscriptions, no data collection.

**What it does:**
- Set reminders by countdown (30 min), moment (tonight), or recurring (every 2 hours)
- Share web pages from Safari to remind yourself to read later
- Home screen widgets for quick access
- Snooze or complete reminders right from the notification

**What it doesn't do:**
- No task management, projects, or kanban boards
- No cloud sync (everything local)
- No social features

It's intentionally simple. Built for people who just want "remind me about this later" without the complexity.

Free on the App Store: https://apps.apple.com/us/app/snooze-it-quick-reminders/id6760223190
